2018-2019 UGA Football Season Outlook

Following a stelluar year in Georgia football, the Bulldogs are out to seek vengence and revenge. Following a heart-wrenching loss to Alabama in the final seconds of the BCS National Championship, the Bulldogs are back with a hunger for the title.Last season the Bulldogs went 13-2, a stellular record on its own, however it serves as only the minimum for the Bulldogs in the 2018 season.If the dawgs want to be as dominant as they were last year, there are major issues to acknowledge and fix. The biggest and most difficult one would be finding replacements for the dynamic duo of Nick Chubb and Sony Michel. Both Chubb and Michael broke numerous records while at Georgia, therefore whoever replaces them, expectations are high. Another major issue that needs fixing, is finding replacements from defensive MVP and Bullake Award winner Roquan Smith. Smith lead the teams in tackles and was a leader on defense, making him very hard to replace. For the Bulldogs the 2018-19 season means winning it all or nothing.

Uga 2018-2019 Roster

Luckily for the UGA Bulldogs, the task of winning it all is very doable given the current roster and recruiting class of 2019. Not only does UGA bring back 7 players on offense and 9 players on defense, they also have the number one recruting class in the nation. During the offseason Kirby Smart, managed to grab four 5 star recruits, ten 4 star recruits, and twelve 3 star recruits, giving UGA one of its best recruiting class in history. Both the remaining and upcoming roster are packed with talent, however that may not be enough.The biggest name from the freshman class, and possibly the next playmaker we should all be watching goes by the name of Justin Fields. Justin is a the number two ranked quarterback in the nation and the top rank dual-threat qb. All eyes will be on him next year as he tries to compete with sophomore quarterback Jake Fromm.
